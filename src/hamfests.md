### Planning to Attend?

**Tips for Ham-Fest Success**:
- Arrive early for best selection
- Bring cash (many sellers don't take cards)
- Bring a bag or cart for purchases
- Test equipment if possible before buying
- Network and make new friends
- Attend seminars to learn something new
- Don't forget your business cards or QSL cards

### Table Rental Information

Most ham-fests offer table rentals for sellers. Contact the individual event organizers for pricing and availability.
