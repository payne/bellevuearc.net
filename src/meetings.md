## Monthly Meetings

Our club holds regular monthly meetings where members gather to discuss amateur radio topics, share projects, and plan upcoming activities.

### Meeting Schedule

**When**: First Thursday of every month
**Time**: 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
**Location**: Community Center, Main Hall
*123 Radio Street, Your City, ST 12345*

### What to Expect

#### General Meeting (7:00 PM - 7:45 PM)
- Welcome and introductions
- Club business and announcements
- Committee reports
- Old and new business
- Upcoming events and activities

#### Program/Presentation (7:45 PM - 8:30 PM)
Each month features a different topic or presentation, including:
- Technical demonstrations
- Guest speakers
- Equipment reviews
- Operating techniques
- Contest strategies
- Emergency preparedness

#### Social Time (8:30 PM - 9:00 PM)
- Informal discussions
- Q&A sessions
- Elmering opportunities
- Refreshments

### Visitors Welcome

All meetings are open to the public. Whether you're a licensed operator, studying for your license, or just curious about amateur radio, you're welcome to attend. No reservation required!

### Virtual Attendance

Can't make it in person? Join us via our club repeater or online video conference. Contact a club officer for connection details.

### Special Meetings

In addition to our monthly general meetings, we also hold:
- **Board Meetings**: Third Tuesday of each month (Members welcome)
- **VE Test Sessions**: Second Saturday of each month (By appointment)
- **Technical Workshops**: As scheduled (Check calendar)
