## Welcome to the Aksarben Amateur Radio Club (AARC) fan site

Welcome to our community of radio enthusiasts! We are dedicated to promoting amateur radio operations, education, and fellowship among operators of all skill levels.

This site is a suplement to [Ak-Sar-Ben Amateur Radio Club](https://www.aksarbenarc.org/home) official website.

### What We Do

- **Monthly Meetings**: Join us for regular meetings where we discuss projects, share knowledge, and plan activities
  * Meetings are open to the public and are held on the second Friday of most months at the [American Red Cross, 2912 S 80th Ave](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Xe3JpAe12gfF9BQp8). Park and enter on the west side of the building. Meetings start at 7 pm.  Technical presentation and disussion starts at 7:30 pm.
  * Board meetings are open to the public.  They take place at 6 pm on the last Tuesday of each month at 76th & Cass St. Hy-Vee in the Cafe 
- **Community Service**: We participate in public service events and [Douglas County Nebraska
ARES/SKYWARN](https://dcaresne.wixsite.com/dcaresne)
- **Education**: We offer licensing classes and technical workshops for operators at all levels
- **Social Events**: Connect with fellow hams at our social gatherings, field day operations, and [Groups.io email forum](https://aksarbenarc.groups.io/g/main/topics)
