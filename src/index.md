## Welcome to the Amateur Radio Club

Welcome to our community of radio enthusiasts! We are dedicated to promoting amateur radio operations, education, and fellowship among operators of all skill levels.

### What We Do

- **Monthly Meetings**: Join us for regular meetings where we discuss projects, share knowledge, and plan activities
- **Community Service**: We provide emergency communications support and participate in public service events
- **Education**: We offer licensing classes and technical workshops for operators at all levels
- **Social Events**: Connect with fellow hams at our social gatherings and field day operations

### Getting Started

Whether you're a seasoned operator or just curious about amateur radio, we welcome you to explore our site and learn more about our activities. Use the menu to navigate to different sections.

For more information about joining or participating in club activities, please attend one of our monthly meetings or contact us through our website.
