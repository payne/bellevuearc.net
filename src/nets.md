### NET Etiquette

**How to Check In**:
1. Listen first to understand the format
2. Wait for Net Control to call for check-ins
3. Give your callsign clearly (phonetics optional)
4. State if you have traffic or just checking in
5. Be brief and courteous

**General Guidelines**:
- Use proper operating procedures
- Keep transmissions concise
- No rag-chewing during formal nets
- Turn off CTCSS/PL squelch to hear all stations
- Stand by if you miss your turn
- Thank Net Control when you sign off

- **CW Traffic NET**: 3.570 MHz, Daily 7:00 PM local
- **Maritime Mobile NET**: 14.300 MHz, Daily 12:00 PM ET
